Research Areas

Our main directions of research cover many aspects of String Theory, Supersymmetry and Supergravity. In particular, these include:
  • String Theory Compactifications
  • String Cosmology
  • Supersymmetric Field Theories
  • Black Hole Physics
  • Beyond Standard Model Physics
  • Alternative Formulation of Quantum Mechanics
and other topics aimed at making the connection between String Theory and the real world.
